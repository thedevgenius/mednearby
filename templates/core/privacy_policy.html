Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

MedNearby respects your privacy. We do not collect personal information from general users such as name, phone number, email address, or medical information.

Location Information

MedNearby may request access to your device location to:

  • Show healthcare businesses near you
  • Calculate distance from your location
  • Improve nearby search results

Your location is used only to provide location-based features and is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Location Permission

Location access is optional. You can deny or disable location permission at any time through your browser or device settings.

Some nearby search features may not work properly without location access.

Business Information

We may collect information from businesses, clinics, doctors, pharmacies, diagnostic centres, hospitals, and other healthcare service providers.

This information may include:

  • Business or professional name
  • Address and location
  • Phone, WhatsApp, and email
  • Business hours and doctor schedules
  • Services, facilities, and amenities
  • Photos, descriptions, offers, and updates

Business information submitted to MedNearby may be displayed publicly on our Platform so that users can discover and contact the business or healthcare professional.

Businesses are responsible for ensuring that the information they provide is accurate, lawful, and up to date.

Third-Party Services

MedNearby may contain links or buttons that open third-party services such as map applications, phone calls, WhatsApp, or business websites.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. MedNearby is not responsible for how those services collect or use information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when required. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
