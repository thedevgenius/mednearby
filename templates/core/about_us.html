About Us

Our story and mission

About MedNearby

MedNearby is a community-focused platform created to help people find nearby doctors, clinics, pharmacies, diagnostic centres, and other essential medical services more easily.

Our purpose is simple: to make local healthcare information easier to discover, especially when someone needs help quickly.

Built to Help the Community

MedNearby is not a conventional profit-focused business. It is a community-help initiative designed to make useful local medical information more accessible.

We want people to spend less time searching and find suitable medical support near them more easily.

We Are Not a Medical Provider

MedNearby does not provide medical treatment, diagnosis, emergency services, or medical advice. We only help people discover and connect with listed medical providers by displaying information submitted by providers or collected from publicly available sources.

Patients should always contact the medical provider directly to confirm availability, consultation fees, services, timings, qualifications, and other details before visiting.

We Need Provider Support

Running and maintaining MedNearby requires continuous effort, including:

  • Adding and updating medical listings
  • Maintaining the website and mobile platform
  • Improving search and location-based discovery
  • Verifying provider information
  • Expanding the platform to more communities

To continue this initiative, we need support from doctors, clinics, pharmacies, diagnostic centres, hospitals, and other medical providers.

How Medical Providers Can Help

Medical providers can support MedNearby by:

  • Creating or claiming their listing
  • Providing accurate and updated information
  • Informing us when their details change
  • Contributing the one-time support amount
  • Helping patients discover reliable local medical services

Our Commitment

We aim to:

  • Make nearby healthcare information easier to find
  • Keep listings clear, useful, and updated
  • Give medical providers better local visibility
  • Help patients connect directly with providers
  • Avoid unnecessary recurring charges for providers
  • Build a trusted community healthcare directory

MedNearby is built on the belief that access to clear and reliable local healthcare information can make a meaningful difference.

With the support of medical providers and the community, we can make nearby medical help easier to find for everyone.
